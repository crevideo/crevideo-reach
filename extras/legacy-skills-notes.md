# Expert Mode (Skills) — Enablement Notes

> 🇺🇸 **English (this page)** · 🇨🇳 [中文版](./legacy-skills-notes.zh-CN.md)

> Claude ships with a set of **expert modes**. You keep using the prompts from Part 2 of the Handbook as usual; Claude **auto-activates** the matching expert flow behind the scenes — **you don't need to remember names or pick anything manually.**

## What they can do for you
Covers these scenarios (auto-activated by matching prompts): **scoring creators**, deciding **tiers**, **commission & compliance** checks, **copy compliance scanning**, drafting **outreach copy**, **reply triage**, **win-back & pruning**, and **post-run performance diagnosis**.

## How to enable
- **Easiest**: have your Crevideo onboarding contact pre-install (or pre-bundle in the Project) — after that you do nothing.
- **DIY**: Claude Desktop → **Settings → Skills** → add the modules from this directory one by one.
- **Works without it**: the 15-minute First Win and the daily prompts **do not depend on** expert mode — enabling it just makes output more professional, stable, and consistent.

## Optional: tell Claude about your brand (for tighter fit)
The `00-brand-knowledge-pack-template` in this directory lets you fill in your brand voice, words to avoid, and this season's featured SKUs. With it filled, output is more brand-aligned; without it, things still work normally.
